INTERNATIONAL TRAINING PROGRAMME (ITP)

International training programme is a regular feature of SITRAs training activity since 1974 and more than 1400 persons from 72 countries have benefited out of this training over the years. The participants attending these programmes are sponsored by the Ministries of External Affairs, Government of India under the sponsoring scheme, Indian Technical and Economic Co-operation Plan (ITEC). Interested candidates from foreign countries may contact the Indian Embassy/Mission in their Home country for participation details
